Our Story

Founded in 2017 with a vision to provide quality education in Ile-Ife, Noble Stars Academy has grown from humble beginnings to become a premier institution for early childhood and primary education in Osun State.

Our journey began with a simple belief: every child deserves access to excellent education that nurtures their unique talents and prepares them for future success.

Over the years, we've remained committed to our core values of excellence, integrity, and innovation, while continuously improving our facilities and curriculum to meet the evolving needs of our pupils.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape everything we do at Noble Stars Academy.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing, stimulating learning environment that empowers each child to develop intellectually, socially, emotionally, and physically, while instilling strong moral values and a lifelong love of learning.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading educational institution that produces well-rounded, confident, and innovative young individuals who are prepared to excel in their future academic pursuits and make meaningful contributions to society.

Our Core Values

The foundational principles that guide our daily interactions and decisions.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in all aspects of education, from teaching to facilities, ensuring our pupils receive the best possible foundation.

Integrity

We cultivate honesty, responsibility, and strong moral character in our pupils, preparing them to make ethical decisions throughout their lives.

Respect

We foster an environment of mutual respect where diversity is celebrated, and each individual's unique qualities are valued and nurtured.

Innovation

We embrace creative teaching methods and technologies to make learning engaging and relevant to our rapidly changing world.

Community

We build strong partnerships with families and the local community to create a supportive network that enhances our pupils' growth.

Joy

We believe learning should be fun and inspiring, creating an atmosphere where children develop a lifelong love for discovery and knowledge.

Our School Levels

Comprehensive educational programs tailored to each developmental stage.

Creche (6months - 3years)

Our creche provides a safe, nurturing environment for our youngest learners. With a low child-to-caregiver ratio, we focus on sensory stimulation, early motor skills development, and creating a loving atmosphere that feels like a home away from home.

Nursery (Nursery 1 & 2)

Our nursery program introduces structured learning through play. We focus on developing social skills, early literacy, numeracy, and creative expression. The curriculum includes circle time, story sessions, arts and crafts, music, and movement activities.

Kindergarten (KG 1 & 2)

Kindergarten builds foundational academic skills while fostering curiosity and independence. Our program emphasizes phonics-based reading, number concepts, science exploration, and problem-solving. Children begin more structured learning while still enjoying plenty of hands-on activities.

Primary (Primary 1-5)

Our primary program offers a robust curriculum that challenges pupils academically while developing critical thinking and leadership skills. Subjects include English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, ICT, French, Yoruba, and more. We prepare pupils for secondary school entrance exams while nurturing well-rounded individuals.
