Welcome to Noble Stars Academy

Established with a vision to provide quality education, Noble Stars Academy has been nurturing young minds in Ile-Ife since our founding. Our motto "Soaring to Shine and Reign" reflects our commitment to helping each child reach their full potential.

We offer comprehensive educational programs from Creche to Primary 5, with a focus on holistic development that prepares children for the challenges of tomorrow.

Discover More

Our Educational Programs

We provide age-appropriate learning experiences tailored to each developmental stage.

Creche

For our youngest learners (6 months - 2 years), we provide a safe, nurturing environment that supports early development.

Nursery

Our Nursery program (Nursery 1 & 2) focuses on foundational skills through play-based learning and social development.

Kindergarten

Kindergarten 1 & 2 builds literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking skills in preparation for primary education.

Primary

Primary 1-5 offers a robust curriculum that challenges pupils academically while fostering creativity and leadership.
