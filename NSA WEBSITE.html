About Our School

Our Story

Noble Stars Academy was founded with a vision to provide exceptional early childhood and primary education in Ile-Ife, Osun State. Located at No33 Adedoyin Way, beside Old Nitel, Parakin, our institution is committed to nurturing young minds to reach their full potential.

Our motto "Soaring to Shine and Reign" reflects our commitment to helping every child discover their unique talents and develop the skills needed to excel in life.

Our Leadership

Proprietress - Mrs. S.I. Abegunde

Founder - Prof. Abegunde Albert
