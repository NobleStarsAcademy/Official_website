Our Admission Process

Simple steps to enroll your child

1

Complete Inquiry Form

Fill out our online inquiry form on the "contact us" page to express interest and receive detailed information about our programs.

2

School Tour & Assessment

Schedule a visit to our school and have your child participate in a friendly assessment session.

3

Submit Application

Make payment for the school form. Complete the application form and submit required documents along with proof of payment.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or sworn declaration of age
  • 1 recent passport photograph
  • Formal School report card if applicable
  • Proof of payment of ₦5,000 application fee
